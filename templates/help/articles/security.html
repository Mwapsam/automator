Keeping your account secure is mostly about a strong password and the right roles for your team. Manage your own account from Settings.

Update your profile

On Settings → Profile you can change your name and email address. Your email is used for sign-in notices and password resets, so keep it current. (Your username can't be changed.)

Change your password

  1. Go to Settings → Security.
  2. Enter your current password, then your new password twice.
  3. Click Update password — you'll stay signed in.
Choose a long, unique password you don't use anywhere else. We enforce a minimum length and block common or easily-guessed passwords.

Forgot your password?

If you can't sign in, use the Forgot password? link on the sign-in page. We'll email you a secure link to set a new one.

Roles & permissions

Access in a workspace is controlled by roles — Owner, Admin and Member. Give people the least access they need: only owners and admins can manage the team. See Invite & manage your team for the full breakdown.

Good security habits

  • Remove teammates as soon as they leave (Settings → Team).
  • Use admin and member roles rather than sharing one login.
  • Sign out on shared or public computers — there's a Sign out button on the Security page.
  • Keep mailbox passwords distinct from your platform password.
