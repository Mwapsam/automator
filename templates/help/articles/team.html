Workspaces can have as many teammates as you like. Manage everyone from Settings → Team.

Roles

RoleCan do
OwnerEverything, including billing. There is one owner — the person who created the workspace.
AdminManage email (domains, mailboxes, aliases) and manage the team (invite, change roles, remove members).
MemberManage email — domains, mailboxes and aliases — but not the team.

Invite a teammate

  1. Go to Settings → Team (you must be an owner or admin).
  2. Under Invite a teammate, enter their email and pick a role.
  3. Click Send invite. They'll get an email with a secure link.
Invitation links expire after 7 days. If one lapses, just send a new invite.

What the invitee sees

  • If they're new, the link lets them create an account and join in one step.
  • If they already have an account with that email, they're asked to sign in to accept.
  • If they're already signed in, they just confirm and join.

Manage members

  • Change a role — use "Make admin" / "Make member" next to a member.
  • Remove a member — the trash icon revokes their access immediately.
  • Revoke a pending invite — under "Pending invitations", click Revoke.

The owner can't be removed or have their role changed, and you can't remove yourself. To hand over ownership, contact support.
